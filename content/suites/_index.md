We have five luxurious guest suites at our bed and breakfast. Each room has the following amenities:

*  king bed
*  en-suite bath with heated floors
*  two-person soaking tub
*  gas fireplace
*  separate shower
*  private deck
*  spa robes
*  individual climate control
*  wi-fi
*  50 inch television
*  USB ports
