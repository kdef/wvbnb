We have five luxurious guest suites at our bed and breakfast. Each room has the following ammenities:

*  king bed
*  en-suite bath with heated floors
*  two-person soaking tub
*  separate shower
*  patio
*  gas fireplace
*  climate control
*  mini fridge
*  high-definition television
*  wi-fi
